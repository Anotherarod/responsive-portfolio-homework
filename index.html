About me

I was born in Madrid, Spain. My family moved to the United States when I was about 6 years old. Our family comes from a background of culinary culture and diversity. My mother was the first person in all of Spain to open a tanning salon in Madrid during the mid 80's. After she sold her business and we moved to Arlington, Virginia we opened a bakery where we specialized in an 20 layer Austrian style cake. We catered many weddings and were features in a prominent Arlington newspaper for our culinary twist.
